Corporate Social Responsibility

Philosophy

Emcure believes that an organization can only succeed by being socially conscious and participative towards the holistic community and societal development as we coexist and collaborate to grow together. Hence, we ensure our active and evolved participation towards Corporate Social Responsibility.

Programs & initiatives

Education

  • We are partnering with ‘Teach for India’ initiative through sponsorships and volunteering activities such as conduct of academic sessions, provision of soft skill training and provision of technical & vocational trainings at schools.
  • Emcure works closely with various NGOs such as Navkshitij and Gurukul Orphanage among others to promote and support talent development in underprivileged children.

Diabetes Initiatives

  • Diabetes Detection Initiative
  • Emcure has also undertaken country-wide diabetes detection initiatives in collaboration with National Diabetes, Obesity and Cholesterol Disease Foundation, India (N-DOC) with the aim to create awareness around diabetes and obesity among school children, their parents, teachers and general public

Environment

  • We support various environmental initiatives led by eCoexist and Swach, two of the prominent non-profit organizations operating in this space in Pune
  • In the past, we have organized clean-up drives where volunteers have assisted in collection of waste from Pimpri, Sanghvi and Aundh Ghats after the Ganeshostsav

Health

  • 12 * 12 Initiative
    • Our goal with the 12 X 12 initiative is to ensure that every girl of age 12 years has a hemoglobin level of 12 grams, which is considered to be the optimum level for females in that age group. Since the inception of this initiative in 2007, we have been able to aide over 70,000 females across India
    • Anemia detection camps are organized every year to reach out and create awareness among as many people as possible. Just in the last one year, we were able to set-up around 5000 camps across the country which screened close to 60,000 patients for Anaemia
  • Village Adoption Program
    • Village adoption program is another unique initiative of ours, under which a village is adopted by Emcure Pharmaceuticals to conduct free hemoglobin tests and health check-ups, and also provide free samples of medication to Anaemic patients

HIV Initiatives

  • Taal Initiative
  • We have set-up the Treatment Adherence Advocacy and Literacy program in association with ‘the positive network’ in Maharashtra to provide care and support to PLHIVs

Joy of Giving

  • We have celebrated the ‘Joy of Giving’ week that received great response from the employees. As part of this initiative one year, Emcure organized a blood donation camp which received participation from over 180 employees
  • Emcure also organized cloth collection drive, the proceeds of which were donated to the non- profit organization, Jagruti Seva Sansthan

Impact Made

We have a dedicated responsibility towards our CSR initiatives that are integral to our values and have been able to positively impacted lot of organizations, People, beneficiaries and community to create a sustainable value to the society in general.
