Dr. Deepak Gondaliya

President, Manufacturing of our company

As part of this role, Mr. Gondaliya’s priorities are Environment, Health & Safety Management, implementation of Good Manufacturing Practices and Process Efficiency with lean manufacturing.

Deepak focuses on advancing scientific breakthrough leadership in Drug development for complex generic, injectables and complex manufacturing processes with strategic end – to end priorities on company’s portfolio, manufacturing infrastructures and deliverables.

Prior to Emcure, he served reputed pharmaceutical industries in India where he held positions of responsibility of Developing products for global markets. Deepak holds a Ph.D. in Pharmacy (Pharmaceutics and Pharmaceutical Technology).

Deepak is a named inventor on more than 20 patents and has published 5 articles in international journals, with contributions in Novel Drug Delivery Systems and Process. His astute leadership qualities are standout among his peers and won “The Best Manager Award” from The Times of India Ascent conclave too.
