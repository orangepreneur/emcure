Emcure Pharmaceuticals manufactures Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (API) at its Kurkumbh & Pune plant.

This has enabled Emcure to be vertically integrated allowing control over the supply chain and lending flexibility to its production.

Emcure constantly works towards synthesizing molecules of APIs in cost-effective and robust manner through its talented research and development team which focuses on working on innovative concepts.
