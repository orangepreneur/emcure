The Indian Pharmaceutical Market (IPM) is the largest business unit for Emcure Pharmaceuticals, which is operated under Emcure India and Zuventus Healthcare

Despite being a late entrant in the market, Emcure has leapfrogged its peers and established itself as a leading player with a niche product basket and positioning. Emcure achieved a major milestone in April 2021 as it was ranked as the 9th largest company in the Indian pharma market (AICOD-AWACS April-21).

The product basket of Emcure Pharmaceuticals which has a strong chronic and sub-chronic focus, covers all the major therapies and is the market leader in areas such as women's health, blood-related, HIV, anti-infectives, cardiac and vitamins.

Emcure has been ranked #1 in blood-related, gynecology and HIV-related products consistently for the past three years. Emcure's domestic business has always been driven by technology-based innovation having launched multiple first to market launches including Hamsyl, Elaxim, Ferium XT, Instgra, Encicarb through innovations in the concepts of chirality, complex molecules, iron compounds and injectables.
