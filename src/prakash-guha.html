Mr. Prakash Guha

Managing Director, Zuventus Healthcare Ltd, a subsidiary of Emcure

He has 37 years’ professional experience having held many leadership positions in the Indian Pharmaceutical Industry.

Zuventus under his leadership created many benchmarks in the India Pharmaceutical Industry. Currently the company holds leadership position in multiple brands and within therapies in the Branded Generics space.

Prakash Kumar has earlier worked with Alkem Laboratories as the President - Sales and Marketing and at Wander Limited as Deputy Managing Director.

Prakash Kumar is a well known philanthropist and is associated with many voluntary organisations primarily into uplifting down trodden and orphans. He is a recipient of multiple awards like the “Rajiv Gandhi Shiromani Award” for outstanding contribution to the Nation and the “Rashtriya Udyog Ratna Award”. He is a graduate in science from Utkal University.
