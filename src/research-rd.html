Emcure Pharma R&D Centre locations

The R&D activities of Emcure Pharmaceuticals are spread across five dedicated facilities, located in Pune (Bhosari, Hinjawadi, Pimpri) and Ahmedabad. Of the five R&D centers, two are focused on API research, four on formulations research and one on biotechnology research.
