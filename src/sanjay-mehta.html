Mr. Sanjay Mehta

President, Commercial of our company

He’s been associated with the company since 1989. He is on the Board of Zuventus Healthcare & of Emcure’s International Subsidiaries. His responsibilities also include overseeing the RoW markets and the procurement.

Sanjay has shaped the company’s commercial structure with years of service & ingeniousness and helped the organization achieve a seamless supply chain, continuous improvements in productivity & smooth distribution of finished products. He has also played a crucial role in devising the strategy for RoW markets enabling Emcure to enter new markets and deepen market penetration.

Sanjay is a Commerce Graduate from the Pune University.
