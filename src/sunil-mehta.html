Mr. Sunil Mehta

Executive Director, Projects of our Company

He has been associated with the company since October 1983 and joined the Board in 2013. He also serves on the board of Gennova Biopharmaceuticals Ltd.

Mr. Mehta has played a vital role in setting up Emcure Group’s State of the Art Infrastructure and Manufacturing Facilities. While doing so, he has ensured that every facility is modern, has the right mix of sophisticated equipment and automation, is environment friendly, complies with all protocols and guidelines and most importantly, safeguards the health and safety of our employees. He is also actively involved in the company’s Corporate Social Responsibility activities.

He is a graduate in Commerce from the Pune University and a postgraduate in Business Administration from the Institute of Management Development and Research, Pune.
