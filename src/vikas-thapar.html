Mr. Vikas Thapar

President - Corporate Development, Strategy & Finance of our company

He has a proven track record with 23 years of work experience and expertise in providing value-added business and financial support to multiple companies across different industries.

Vikas has been an integral part of the Emcure management team for the last 15 years. He initially looked at Business Development and later his role expanded to include Corporate Strategy and Finance. He has played a crucial role in spearheading Emcure’s growth in Europe and Canada. Vikas has led Emcure’s inorganic growth strategies over the years and has spearheaded acquisitions of various companies, brands and product portfolios. He has ensured the seamless integration and scale up of critical acquisitions helping the organization in establishing a global footprint. He has also driven Emcure’s various fundraising efforts.

Prior to joining Emcure, Vikas worked in the Silicon Valley, USA with Ebay/PayPal as well as Agilent Technologies where he worked in Finance.

Vikas holds a bachelor’s degree in Management Science from the University of California, San Diego. He did his MBA from the USC Marshall School of Business of University of Southern California.
